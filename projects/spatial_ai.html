We focus on 3D spatial modeling, one of the key technologies for AR applications, robotics, and computational photography.

We aim at building virtual representations of real indoor environments. These representations can be exported to a virtual simulator and used to train and test vision-based methods.

We employ multi-modal visual recognition to produce a detailed semantic map of the environment and implement inventive approaches of combining data of various types and origins within a single semantic mapping pipeline.

As a part of this project, we also work on a capturing system for geometric reconstruction of large-scale indoor environments.
