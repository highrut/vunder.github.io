#object_recognition

We focus on 3D spatial modeling which is one of the key technologies for AR applications, robotics and computational photography.

The main goal of our team is the development of new visual mapping and navigation technologies. The mapping technologies will allow building a virtual representation of real indoor environments, which will enable visual-based navigation inside such environments.

The navigation technologies will provide a tool for localization inside an indoor environment from visual observation, which can be used to enable indoor navigation for mobile users or navigation and planning for indoor robots.

The virtual representation of mapped environment can also be exported to a virtual simulator, and used for training and testing of vision-based methods.

We also work on a capturing system for geometric reconstruction of large-scale indoor environments, semantic-based recognition of the scene to produce detailed semantic map, handling transparent and reflective surfaces, visual-based localization from a mobile phone.
