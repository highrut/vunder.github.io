Anna Vorontsova

Engineer @ VUNDER

a.vorontsova@samsung.com

Hi, I'm Anna. I have been a member of VUNDER Lab since 2018 when I completed my bachelor's degree and enrolled in my master's degree. Two years later I wrote my master's thesis on visual odometry summarizing my projects at VUNDER. In addition to visual odometry, I also worked on visual localization. Currently, I'm focusing on object recognition.
